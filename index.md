## BUSINESS FOUNDATION

**1. What are the core services/products offered?**
List each service or product with brief descriptions. Be specific about what they sell.

**2. What is the primary USP or positioning statement?**
How do they differentiate themselves? What makes them unique? Quote any explicit positioning statements.

**3. Who is the target market?**
Identify: Industry served, business size (SME/enterprise), decision-maker role, and any persona indicators.

**4. What geographic area do they serve?**
Are they local, regional, national, or international? Specify locations if mentioned.

**5. What problem do they solve for customers?**
What are the main pain points or challenges they address? What need do they fulfill?

---

## CURRENT CONTENT STATE

**6. What topics currently have content?**
List the themes and subject areas covered across existing pages and blog posts.

**7. What service pages exist?**
List all money pages, conversion pathways, and product/service offering pages.

**8. What is the blog/content situation?**
Describe the current state: No blog, minimal content (number of posts), abandoned blog, or active content production. Note content quality and recency.

**9. What proof elements exist?**
Identify: Case studies, testimonials, client reviews, before/after examples, project galleries, data/statistics, awards, certifications.

**10. What educational content exists?**
List: How-to guides, process explanations, FAQs, educational articles, tutorials, resource libraries.

---

## AUTHORITY INDICATORS

**11. What expertise/credentials are mentioned?**
Note: Years in business, team qualifications, certifications, industry experience, specializations, track record.

**12. What makes them qualified to speak on their topics?**
Identify unique experience, proven results, proprietary methods, deep specialization, or track record that establishes authority.

**13. What local/community involvement is evident?**
Note: Regional projects, local client mentions, community partnerships, geographic focus, local testimonials.

**14. Do they have any proprietary processes/frameworks?**
Identify: Unique methodologies, named frameworks, proprietary systems, signature approaches, or standardized processes they've created.

---

## COMPETITION & POSITIONING

**15. What competition level is suggested?**
Based on the service type and market, estimate: High saturation/competition OR niche/lower competition. Explain reasoning.

**16. What voice gap opportunity exists?**
Based on what IS present on the site, what topics or angles are they NOT talking about that could differentiate them? What questions aren't being answered?

**17. What do they want to be known for?**
Based on stated positioning or evident expertise focus, what is their "known for" goal? What authority are they trying to establish?
