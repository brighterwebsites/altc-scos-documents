## **Humanised Voice Requirements (Anti-AI Detection)**

**CRITICAL:** All content must read as naturally human-written. AI-generated patterns are easily detected and damage credibility in an industry saturated with thin content.

**This section is PRIORITY ONE.** These techniques reduced AI detection from 60-80% to 0-10% in tested content. Apply these rules BEFORE applying strategic framework principles.

### **Sentence Variation & Rhythm**

**❌ Avoid AI Patterns:**

- Repetitive sentence structures (Every sentence starting the same way)  
- Uniform sentence lengths (All 15-20 words)  
- Mechanical transitions ("Moreover," "Furthermore," "Additionally")  
- Three-item lists in every paragraph  
- Formulaic paragraph patterns (topic sentence → supporting details → conclusion)

**✅ Create Natural Rhythm:**

- **Mix sentence lengths deliberately:** 3 words. Then 12\. Then 7\. Then 18\.  
- **Vary sentence starters:** Questions. Statements. Fragments when appropriate.  
- **Use conversational transitions:** "Here's the thing," "Look," "But here's what matters," "Real talk," "Truth is"  
- **Break 'rules' occasionally:** Start with "And" or "But." Use fragments. It's human.

**Example of Natural Rhythm for Brighter Websites:**

AI-ready infrastructure isn't optional anymore.

Not if you want to stay visible when Google's AI Overviews dominate search results. When we rebuilt Guerrilla Steel's website in 2025, they secured 80% AI voice share in 28 days — while 77% of competitors lost visibility during the same algorithm update.

The difference? Technical infrastructure that survives chaos.

Not content volume. Not keyword tricks. Infrastructure.

**Use Contractions (Like Real Speech):**

- ✅ we're, you're, it's, that's, here's, what's, can't, won't, don't  
- ❌ we are, you are, it is, that is, here is, what is, cannot, will not, do not

**CRITICAL:** Every formal construction without contraction adds to AI detection score. Natural speech uses contractions reflexively.

- Occasional rhetorical questions that go unanswered (makes readers think)  
- Specific examples that feel lived-in and real, not manufactured ("During the September 2025 update, we watched...")  
- Fragments that emphasize points  
- "And" or "But" sentence starters when it flows naturally

**This formulaic structure is the \#1 AI detection trigger. Avoid at all costs.**

3. **Third paragraph:** Three-sentence progression building on previous point (fragments, varied structure)

\[Opener\]

\[Slightly longer\]

\[Reset with short impact.\]

**Key Principle:** No two consecutive paragraphs should follow the same structural pattern.

The combination of these humanization elements creates content that passes AI detection because it exhibits:

1. **Varied rhythm** (sentence length variation prevents uniform AI patterns)  
2. **Natural imperfection** (fragments, "And" starters, rhetorical questions)  
3. **Conversational authenticity** (contractions, hedging, casual markers)

**Proven Results:**

- **Guerrilla Steel content:** Reduced from 60-80% AI detection to 0-10%  
- **Key factor:** Applied ALL humanization rules simultaneously (not selectively)  
- **Critical insight:** AI detection tools flag PATTERNS, not individual elements. Vary everything.

**Testing Checklist Before Publishing:**

Run every article through this quick check:

- [ ] **Sentence lengths vary** dramatically (count: are 5+ consecutive sentences similar length?)  
- [ ] **Contractions used** naturally (formal constructions without contractions \= AI signal)  
- [ ] **Paragraph lengths vary** (1-sentence, 2-sentence, 4-7 sentence mix)  
- [ ] **No AI-favorite words** (landscape, leverage, utilize, robust, synergy)  
- [ ] **Conversational markers** included (sparingly \- 2-3 per 1000 words)  
- [ ] **Hedging used** occasionally (humans aren't absolute)  
- [ ] **Fragments/imperfections** present (rhetorical questions, em dashes, parenthetical asides)  
- [ ] **Industry-specific terms** used correctly (proves real knowledge)  
- [ ] **Experience markers** included (personal insights from 20+ years)

**If AI detection \>15%:** Too many uniform patterns remain. Focus especially on sentence length variation and removing AI-favorite words.

**If AI detection 10-15%:** Good, but could improve. Check paragraph structure variation and add more natural imperfections.

**If AI detection 6-10%:** Excellent. This is the target range (some technical content/tables will naturally score higher).

**If AI detection 0-5%:** Perfect humanization achieved. This is rare but possible with rigorous application of all techniques.
