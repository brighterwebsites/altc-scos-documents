## Pre-Prompt Information Gathering Checklist

Before using the prompt, collect these details:

### Essential Information
- [ ] Business name and industry
- [ ] Core services/products (3-5 main offerings)
- [ ] Primary USP or differentiation
- [ ] Target market description
- [ ] Geographic scope (local/regional/national)
- [ ] Main problem they solve
- [ ] Years in business or key credentials
- [ ] Existing tagline/motto (if any)

### Voice Indicators
- [ ] 5-10 direct quotes from their existing content
- [ ] 3-5 phrases from customer reviews
- [ ] How they describe themselves (in their words)
- [ ] What tone they currently use
- [ ] Industry jargon they use naturally
- [ ] What they NEVER want to sound like

### Proof Assets
- [ ] Measurable results (numbers, stats)
- [ ] Types of testimonials/reviews available
- [ ] Case studies or project examples
- [ ] Certifications, awards, recognition
- [ ] Proprietary processes/frameworks

### Competitive Context
- [ ] How competitors typically sound
- [ ] Voice gaps in the market
- [ ] What makes their voice unique
- [ ] Their "known for" positioning goal
